These are separate, more detailed competency pages from the old site structure.
They even link back to ../module.qmd (the old module listing). They are not the 
flip cards you see on the Curriculum page.

The flip cards we now have on the site are hardcoded HTML directly inside curriculum.qmd
(no external files involved). The competency/*.qmd files were part of the old design where:

competency.qmd listed all competency cards (pulling from competency/*.qmd)
Each card linked to a detail page like comp_data_eng.qmd
Those detail pages linked onward to module.qmd
That whole chain (competency.qmd → competency/*.qmd → module.qmd) was replaced by the single 
integrated curriculum.qmd page that has the flip cards + module listings all in one place.
