How the Community Data Lab Works

We connect real-world challenges with student-led data inquiry to generate insights and impact for our community.

1. Partner & Problem

We collaborate with community partners to identify a pressing question or problem that can be explored with data.

2. Data & Analysis

Students work in small groups to analyze relevant datasets, applying data science methods to uncover patterns and insights.

3. Insights & Impact

The findings, materials, and datasets are shared back with the partner and the public to drive decisions and learning.
